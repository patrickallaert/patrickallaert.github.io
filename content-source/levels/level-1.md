# Level 1

Source: `www.sarahforro.com__level1description.html`

Good news! Everyone is welcome: no experience is needed for Level 1.

Level 1 is where we lay the foundations for your forró journey. We want to create good habits that will allow you to learn and grow as a dancer, and correct any “bad habits” which risk holding you back in the future.

Even if - or perhaps especially if! - you have experience of other partner dances, we recommend starting with Level 1. This is where you will learn how forró is both similar and different to other couple dances.
