# Arriving at the Dojo du Brochet

Source: `www.sarahforro.com__arriving-dojo-du-brochet.html`

Thanks for registering for a class or workshop at Dojo du Brochet (rue du Brochet 55, Ixelles!

This is a gem of a dance room, but the entrance is a little hidden so here's a short video to ensure you don't get lost.

If the main door is open, come in, take your shoes off in the entrance hall and join us in the first room on the left.

If the main door is closed, please ring the doorbell on the right hand side of the door and someone will buzz you in. If there is music playing, you might need to ring twice!

It's not allowed to use street shoes in the dance room: you can dance barefoot, in socks or in dance shoes which you use exclusively indoors.
