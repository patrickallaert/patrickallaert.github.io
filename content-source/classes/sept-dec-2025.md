PROGRAMME Take a look at the image above for a quick and easy overview of the programme.

Never tried forró before? Or you'd like to meet us before registering? We will have lots of free taster classes scheduled during the summer and in September: check out the dates here.

Each trimester includes a "special" course or two to go deeper into a topic...

Thanks to your enthusiasm for the topic, we continue our Roots offering! To register for this course, leaders and followers need to master the trava, sacada and paulista.

And we're excited to add two new short courses! These are 5-week courses which zoom in on specific parts of our dance world and are highly recommended as a bonus alongside your regular class (although you can also register for them as a standalone option).

1) Hands Free is for students in Level 3 and above. In this course, the leaders will develop their ability to lead with different points of contact, while the followers explore how to use their arms comfortably and expressively! We made a little video to give you a better idea of what this involves, check it out here.

2) Creating Leader Variations is an everyone-leads-and-everyone-follows course. In this course, everyone will learn how to create new variations of figures to diversify your leading and practise following them. To participate, you need to have completed Level 2 as a leader AND be able to follow vai e vem, pião, facão, balanço and xaxado.

And one final more piece of good news for this trimester: we're adding a mid-week prática! Join us at Salle Dublin on Wednesdays from 20h15-22h to get an extra dose of forró. Contribution-based, just like on Sundays, we recommend about 3-4 euros per person to help us cover the cost of the room.
