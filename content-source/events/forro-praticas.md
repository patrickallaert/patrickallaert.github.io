# Forró Práticas

Source: `www.sarahforro.com__forr--pr-ticas.html`

Join us for our forró "prática": This is an opportunity to dance, practice and reconnect.

We now have TWO práticas per week: **Sundays** and **Wednesdays**!

- No partner needed.
- No registration required.
- Special workshops from time to time. Programme on workshops and initiations pages.
