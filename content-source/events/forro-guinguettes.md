## Forró Guinguettes - Every Thursday!

Summer has come, and with it, a return to the Forró Guinguettes!

On these long summer evenings, there is nothing better than a dance to Brazilian rhythms in excellent company and a beautiful green environment.

And if you don't dance forró YET, fear not! We will be teaching a free initiation class at 19h30 every two weeks. See the schedule for the initiations here.
