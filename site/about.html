School and community

Conexão teaches Forró in Brussels with a strong link between classes and social dancing.

The school identity is warm, community-led, and practical. The new site keeps that energy, but presents information in a way that works better for reading, registering, and returning later.

What matters here

  • clear levels
  • regular weekly rhythm
  • room to practice
  • community as part of learning

Teaching

Conexão teaches in recurring term cycles, making space for both structured classes and practice time through the week.

Community

The school is not only about technical progression. Socials, initiations, and recurring community moments are part of the same ecosystem.

Brussels-based
