Classes

Conexão runs regular Forró classes in Brussels on a trimester rhythm: January to March, April to June, and September to December.

  • Fair trade flexi-trimester: 120 euros

Registration

You do not need a partner to register. Conexão balances leaders and followers across the groups.

  1. Fill in the registration form.
  2. Make payment straight away to the bank account details provided.
  3. You will receive a confirmation email once the payment reaches the bank account.
