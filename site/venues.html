Venues

Conexão uses several different venues in Brussels. This page gathers the addresses together with the notes that are useful before arriving.

GC Ten Noey

Address:
Rue de la Commune 25
1210 Saint-Josse-ten-Noode

A lovely-sized dance room in a cool cultural centre.

OpenStreetMap · Google Maps

Dojo du Brochet

Address:
Rue du Brochet 55
1050 Ixelles

With the smoothest floor around.

This is a gem of a dance room, but the entrance is a little hidden.

OpenStreetMap · Google Maps

  • If the main door is open, come in, take your shoes off in the entrance hall and join us in the first room on the left.
  • If the main door is closed, ring the doorbell on the right hand side of the door and someone will buzz you in.
  • It is not allowed to use street shoes in the dance room: dance barefoot, in socks, or in dance shoes used exclusively indoors.
